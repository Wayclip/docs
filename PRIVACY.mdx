# Privacy Policy For Wayclip

**Last Updated: September 2025**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Wayclip ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our software and services (collectively, the "Services"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

For any privacy-related questions or requests, you can contact us at **privacy@wayclip.com**.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect a limited amount of information necessary to provide and improve our Services.

### 2.1. Information You Provide to Us
*   **Account Information:** When you register for an account, we collect information such as your email address and any information provided through third-party authentication services you connect, like GitHub.

### 2.2. Information We Collect Automatically
*   **Technical Information:** To ensure the security and functionality of our Services, we may collect your IP address and a Hardware ID (HWID). This helps us prevent account abuse and diagnose technical issues.
*   **User Content:** We collect and store the video clips and associated metadata that you upload to our servers to provide you with our cloud storage and sharing features.

### 2.3. Information from Third Parties
*   **Payment Information:** We use Stripe to process payments for our premium subscriptions. We do not collect or store your full credit card information. This information is provided directly to Stripe, and its use is governed by Stripeâ€™s Privacy Policy.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

*   **To Provide and Maintain the Services:** We use your account information and User Content to operate our platform, host your clips, and provide you with the features of your chosen subscription tier.
*   **To Communicate With You:** We use your email address to send you service-related notifications, security alerts, and information about your account.
*   **For Security and Fraud Prevention:** We use technical information like your IP address and HWID to protect our Services from abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.
*   **To Process Payments:** We work with Stripe to securely process your subscription payments.

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We will not share your information with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

*   **With Service Providers:** We share information with our payment processor, Stripe, solely for the purpose of processing your subscription payments.
*   **For Legal Reasons:** We may disclose your information if we believe that it is reasonably necessary to comply with a law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal data as long as your account is active.

If you choose to delete your account, we offer a 14-day recovery period. After these 14 days, we will permanently delete all your personal information and User Content from our production systems.

You may also delete individual clips at any time from your dashboard.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you. You can manage most of your information through your account dashboard. For any requests you cannot fulfill yourself, please contact us at **privacy@wayclip.com**.

## 7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet-based service is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or through a notification on your user dashboard before the changes take effect.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please email us at **privacy@wayclip.com**.
